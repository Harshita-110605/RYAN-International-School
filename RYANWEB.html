Welcome to Ryan International School

Inspiring Excellence, Shaping Futures

At Delhi Public School, we are dedicated to nurturing young minds and fostering a love for learning that transcends the classroom. Established with a vision to provide holistic education, DPS has been at the forefront of academic excellence, innovation, and character building.


Our Mission


Our mission is to cultivate an environment where students are encouraged to discover their passions, develop critical thinking skills, and become lifelong learners. We strive to equip our students with the knowledge, values, and competencies necessary to excel in a rapidly changing world.


Our Values


At DPS, we uphold a set of core values that guide our educational philosophy:


  1. Integrity: We believe in honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in all our endeavors.
  2. Excellence: We are committed to the highest standards of academic and personal achievement.
  3. Respect: We foster an environment of mutual respect, inclusivity, and diversity.
  4. Innovation: We encourage creativity and embrace new ideas to enhance learning experiences.
  5. Community: We build strong, supportive relationships within our school and with our broader community.

Academic Programs


Our comprehensive academic programs are designed to challenge and inspire students at every level. From our early childhood education to our rigorous high school curriculum, we offer a wide range of subjects and extracurricular activities to cater to diverse interests and talents.


Facilities and Resources


Our state-of-the-art facilities provide a conducive environment for learning and growth. Our classrooms are equipped with modern technology, and our extensive library, science labs, sports complexes, and arts centers support a well-rounded education.


Achievements and Recognition


DPS is proud of its long-standing tradition of academic excellence and its reputation as one of the leading educational institutions in the country. Our students consistently achieve top ranks in national and international examinations, and our alumni have gone on to excel in various fields globally.


Join Us


We invite you to explore our website and learn more about what makes DPS a unique and inspiring place for education. Whether you are a prospective student, parent, or educator, we welcome you to join our vibrant community and be a part of our journey towards shaping the leaders of tomorrow.
